Biography

I am currently an Algorithm Researcher at Alibaba Group. My research interests lie at the intersection of Reinforcement Learning (RL), Large Language Models (LLMs), and Agentic Systems.

I have been leading and contributing to several influential projects, including ROME (an open-source agentic model ecosystem) and ROLL/ROLL Flash (high-performance RL training frameworks). My goal is to build autonomous agents that can reason, self-refine, and interact effectively with complex real-world environments.
